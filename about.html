About Me

My name is Michael McAdow and I was born, raised and currently live in Columbus, Ohio. Among my interests are computers/technology, guitar, synthesizers and drum machines, traveling and good food. My loves are my family, friends and dogs.

Professionally, I have worked for many years for Flex-Core, and electrical company. At Flex-Core I work in a team oriented environment to track and fulfill customer orders. In recent years I have helped to transition the inventory of our products at the company to be tracked via IQMS.

In 2018, after a conversation with a friend who is a Software Developer, I decided to explore more about coding and development. I began learning to code little projects in my free time and found that coding filled me with a sense of satisfaction and optimism. Then in 2019 I made the decision to return to school and in 2022 I obtained my Web Developer degree from Columbus State Community College.

Since then, I have continued studying and developing my free time and am immensely excited to begin my career as a Web Developer.
